Prof. Pankaj Jalote's opinion about the programme
Professor Pankaj Jalote
The mentoring program at IIIT-D, fills a very real need, in helping first year students cope with the stresses and complexities of campus life. Wrenched from the comfort zone of family and high school, many first years find it tough adapting to the rigors and demands of life at a major tech. university. The mentor program is designed to provide first year students with ready access to one on one and group counseling.
Professor Raj Ayyar
Mentoring by senior students is significant as the student mentor is both a friend and a role model who supports and encourages a younger partner in his/her academic and personal growth. The mentor is also a guide who helps a young person make the difficult change from adolescence to young adult . This time of transition is especially important, for it is a time when young people are making decisions about various work related issues which seem complicated initially and they require the presence of their seniors as friend, philosopher and guide.
Ms. Amita Puri
