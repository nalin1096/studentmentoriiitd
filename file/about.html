About The Team

A Few Details

The Mentoring program, inspired by Counselling services at IIT Kanpur, was successfully launched at IIIT-D, in the Monsoon semester 2014. The Mentoring program is designed to help first year students deal with the stress of adapting to a university campus. Mentors are expected to play friend, philosopher and guide to first year students in a variety of ways.

The mind is not a vessel to be filled, but a fire to be kindled.

-Plutarch

A Few More Details

The mentors are trained in basic counselling strategies by the Institute Counsellor, and this training will be reinforced periodically in booster sessions. We are in the process of developing a Mentor Program web page, and assessment instruments, esp. regular evaluations.
