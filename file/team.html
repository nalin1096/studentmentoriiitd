Professor Raj Ayyar
Faculty Coordinator
       Ms. Amita Puri
       Student counsellor
The faculty coordinator is the 'meta-mentor'.He needs to be freely accessible to mentees and mentors alike, to help them with issues coming out of the mentoring program.
The faculty coordinator needs to be an empathic counselor, assisting mentees and mentors with personal and academic problems and crises, regardless of whether they have found other avenues of help, or not.
The faculty coordinator works with the institute counseling services at all times, and arranges regular mentor trainings and 'booster' training sessions.
The institute Counselor works closely with the Faculty Coordinator in setting up the following:
  • Mentor training for all new mentors.
  • 'Booster' training sessions for mentors through the semester.
  • Special life coach sessions for the mentees.
  • Availability at all times, to intervene in mentee crises, esp. when alerted by a mentor, or by the faculty coordinator.
